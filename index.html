About Us

Pacxone International provides Complete Solutions through the application of resourceful & cost-effective Process Instruments. We are the prime wholesale and trading company for industrial process instruments, machines & accessories supplies, service, maintenance & installation of process & HVAC equipment. We are working in pharma & food markets in Qatar, UAE, East Africa & Pakistan are being our top client markets.

As we have a wide range of Process Control Instruments, a lot of combinations are possible in our existing product range, so, please be in touch with us to get the correct solution for your specific process application Pressure, Differential Pressure, Vacuum, Temperature, Level, Flow, Vibration, Moisture, Conductivity, TDS, and Density.

Apart from the standard range of instruments, we also cover Tailor Made Instrumentation like, Programmable Indicators, Programmable Sequential Timers, Programmable Time Totalizers, Multiple Logic Systems (Many logics in a single unit), Multiple Input with multiple output units, Single Input and Multiple Output units, Bar Graph Indicators, Data Loggers, Process Scanners, Multi-Channel Scanners, PID Controllers, On-Off Controllers, Circular Chart Recorders with MODBUS communication, RS 485 or, RS 232 communications etc.

We believe in teamwork and strictly adhere to high standards of quality. We trust in maintaining long-lasting business relationships.

We fully understand the value of time, money, and resources. Hence, we do our best to supply products that live up to the expectations of our clients. Our conviction towards the industry and our clients has rewarded us with complete, unbridled customer satisfaction. Our mission statement includes:

  • Consistent improvement and development
  • Being up to date with the industry standards at all times
  • Prioritizing our clients, understanding and applying their needs in order to deliver exceptional quality
  • Timely shipments at genuine prices
  • Perfection in even the most minuscule detail of our products
